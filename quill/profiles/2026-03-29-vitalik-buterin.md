# Vitalik Buterin: The Prodigy Who Built Ethereum

## Executive Summary
**Born:** January 31, 1994 (Kolomna, Russia)
**Net Worth:** ~$400 million (2024)
**Known For:** Co-founding Ethereum, youngest crypto billionaire
**Current Role:** Ethereum Foundation Researcher

## The Early Years (1994-2011)
Vitalik Buterin was born in Russia and moved to Canada at age 6. His father, Dmitry Buterin, was a computer scientist who introduced young Vitalik to Bitcoin in 2011. Unlike most teenagers, Vitalik spent his time writing articles about cryptocurrency for Bitcoin Magazine, earning 5 BTC per article (then worth ~$3.50 each).

## The Ethereum Whitepaper (2013)
At age 19, Vitalik published the Ethereum whitepaper in November 2013. His key insight: Bitcoin was a calculator, but the world needed a general-purpose computer. Ethereum would support smart contracts - self-executing code that could power decentralized applications.

**The Innovation:**
- Turing-complete programming language (Solidity)
- Gas fees to prevent infinite loops
- Proof-of-Stake consensus (planned)
- ERC-20 token standard

## The DAO Hack and Resilience (2016)
In June 2016, The DAO (a decentralized venture fund built on Ethereum) was hacked for $60 million. The community faced an existential crisis: return the funds (hard fork) or let the hack stand (code is law).

Vitalik advocated for the hard fork. The community split into Ethereum (ETH) and Ethereum Classic (ETC). Vitalik's leadership during this crisis cemented his status as the benevolent dictator of Ethereum.

## Ethereum 2.0 and The Merge (2022)
The most significant upgrade in blockchain history occurred on September 15, 2022. Ethereum transitioned from Proof-of-Work (mining) to Proof-of-Stake (staking), reducing energy consumption by 99.95%.

**The Numbers:**
- $20+ billion in ETH staked
- 99.95% energy reduction
- 0 downtime during transition
- 400,000+ validators

## Current Focus: L2 Scaling and Privacy
Vitalik now focuses on:
1. **Layer 2 Solutions** - Arbitrum, Optimism, zkSync
2. **Privacy** - Stealth addresses, zero-knowledge proofs
3. **Account Abstraction** - Smart contract wallets
4. **Quantum Resistance** - Preparing for quantum computing threats

## Legacy
Vitalik Buterin transformed from a teenage blogger to the architect of the world's second-largest cryptocurrency. His vision of a "world computer" powers thousands of decentralized applications handling billions in value daily.
