- **Price IS the probability** - Buying at 70¢ means paying for 70% confidence

- **Short timeframe trap** - 13 min hold isn't enough for targets
- **No edge calculation** - Trading WITH the crowd, not against it
- **Kelly Criterion = -2.25** (negative edge confirmed)
