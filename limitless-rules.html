🛡️ RISK MANAGEMENT

Two-Loss Cooldown

After 2 consecutive losses → sit out 3 full cycles (~90 minutes)
Resets streak to zero when cooldown expires. Prevents chasing losses on bad streaks.

Per-Asset Tracking

BTC, ETH, SOL tracked independently
Separate win/loss streaks, separate cooldowns, separate pending bets.
A BTC loss doesn't affect ETH sizing and vice versa.

Capital Protection

Never bet more than ~20% of bankroll per trade
All 3 assets running simultaneously = each capped lower so total exposure stays sane.

✅ GOOD TRADE CRITERIA

The bot looks for windows where multiple layers agree:

  • 60m momentum aligns with short-term direction
  • RSI not overbought/oversold against the direction
  • Price above/below VWAP in our favor
  • OBI confirming (bid-heavy for YES, ask-heavy for NO)
  • Market pricing gives at least 1.2:1 R/R
  • At least 5 minutes left in the window
  • Not in a no-trade zone or ATR lock
When all that lines up, the bot bets.
When it doesn't, it waits — and most windows won't be perfect, which is fine.
