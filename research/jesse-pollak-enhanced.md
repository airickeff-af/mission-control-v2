## 🚀 The Base Creation Story

### 2021: The Pivot Point
- After 5 years at Coinbase, grew consumer team from **3 to 250 people**
- Oversaw Coinbase, Coinbase Pro, and Coinbase Wallet
- Wanted to leave and start his own venture

### Brian Armstrong's Challenge
To retain Pollak, Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong told him:
> **"Go figure out how to bring Coinbase on-chain."**

### The DAO Proposal (That Failed)
Pollak's first idea: Transform Coinbase into a **$1 billion DAO** with 60 employees
- Executive team response: *"We love your energy, but you're using it in the wrong place"*
- Pollak's reaction: Laughed about it later

### 18 Months of Exploration
Explored multiple directions:
1. ❌ DAO concept (rejected)
2. ❌ Advertising market
3. ❌ App store model
4. ❌ Identity applications

**Key Insight:** "First, we need a real software platform."

### Base is Born (August 2023)
- Built on Ethereum as Layer 2
- Aggregates batch transactions
- Q1 2024: **2x Ethereum's transaction volume**
- Q1 2024 Revenue: **$56+ million**
- Major apps: Uniswap, Chainlink, OpenSea
